About me

Need a Creative Product? I can Help You!

Hi! I’m Wasana Dhilshani, and I’m a Graphic designer who has passion for building clean graphic design with intuitive functionalities. I enjoy the process of turning ideas into reality using creative solutions. I’m always curious about learning new skills, tools, and concepts. In addition to working on various solo new innovation, I have worked with creative teams in university, which involves daily stand-ups and communications, source control, and project management. Since a young age, I have had a strong passion for art and have consistently taken part in various art competitions throughout my academic journey, starting from grade school all the way to college.

I possess a degree in Bsc IT to graphic Design for the Enterprise, which has broadened my horizons and helped me to discover various traditional and visual art forms, as well as new artists who have motivated me to be more innovative with my creative abilities. After completing my education, I have gained hands-on experience working in a range of roles as a designer and producer for both public and private organizations. My focus has been on experimenting with different art styles, with a specific emphasis on the areas of rapid communication, advertising, and digital illustration.
